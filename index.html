Winchester Wine and Book Club

Reading books and drinking wine since 2012.

What and Who?

The Winchester Wine and Book Club is a book club based in Winchester, UK.

We are a small(ish) informal and friendly group of people from different professions and of varying ages. We meet once a month to discuss a book, and we take it in turns to chose the book.

Where and When?

We used to meet at the Black Bottle, but sadly that has recently closed down. We are in the process of choosing a new location. TBC.

We meet on the first Wednesday of each month at 7.30pm.
